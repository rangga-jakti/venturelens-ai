Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use VentureLens AI, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name and email address from your Google account when you sign in.
  • Startup Ideas: The text you submit for analysis.
  • Usage Data: Analysis history and app usage patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI-powered startup analysis
  • Save your analysis history
  • Improve our AI models and service quality
  • Send important service updates

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your startup ideas are processed by:

  • Groq API: For AI analysis (LLaMA 3.3 model)

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing data use.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on Railway's infrastructure with PostgreSQL. We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
